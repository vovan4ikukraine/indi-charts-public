# Request Account Deletion - INDI CHARTS

**Application Name**: INDI CHARTS  
**Developer**: INDIGO PRODUCTION

This page provides step-by-step instructions on how to request deletion of your account and personal data from INDI CHARTS.

## How to Request Account Deletion

### Option 1: Delete Account via Email (Recommended)

1. **Send an email** to: **ads.contact.manager@gmail.com**
2. **Subject line**: "Account Deletion Request - INDI CHARTS"
3. **Include the following information**:
   - Your email address associated with the account (the one you used to sign in with Google)
   - Confirmation that you want to delete your account and all associated data

4. **We will respond** within **30 days** to confirm the deletion process

### Option 2: Delete Account via App

1. **Open the INDI CHARTS app** on your device
2. **Navigate to Settings** (usually accessible from the main menu)
3. **Sign out** from your Google account (this removes Firebase authentication)
4. **Clear app data**:
   - **Android**: Go to Settings → Apps → INDI CHARTS → Storage → Clear Data
   - **iOS**: Delete and reinstall the app, or go to Settings → General → iPhone Storage → INDI CHARTS → Delete App
5. **Contact us** via email (ads.contact.manager@gmail.com) to request deletion of cloud-stored data

## What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently deleted:

### Account Information
- ✅ Firebase Authentication data (email address, display name)
- ✅ User ID and account association

### Alert Data
- ✅ All alert rules and configurations
- ✅ Alert history and trigger logs
- ✅ Notification preferences

### Device Information
- ✅ Device registration records
- ✅ Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens
- ✅ Device IDs associated with your account

### Synchronized Data
- ✅ All alerts stored in cloud (Cloudflare D1 database)
- ✅ Watchlist items synchronized to cloud
- ✅ Cross-device synchronization data

### Local Data (when you clear app data)
- ✅ All alert rules stored locally
- ✅ Watchlist items
- ✅ App preferences and settings
- ✅ Cached indicator data

## What Data May Be Retained

The following data may be retained for legal or operational purposes:

### Logs and Analytics
- ⚠️ **Server logs**: May contain IP addresses and request timestamps (retained for up to **90 days** for security and debugging purposes)
- ⚠️ **Error logs**: Crash reports and error logs (retained for up to **90 days** for app improvement)
- ⚠️ **Analytics data**: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics (no personal identifiers)

## Data Deletion Timeline

- **Immediate**: Local app data (when you clear app data or delete the app)
- **Within 30 days**: Cloud-stored data (alerts, device info, account data)
- **Within 90 days**: Server logs and analytics data (automatically purged)

## Partial Data Deletion

If you want to delete only specific data without deleting your entire account:

### Delete Individual Alerts
1. Open the INDI CHARTS app
2. Go to the Alerts screen
3. Swipe left or tap delete on any alert you want to remove
4. The alert will be deleted from both local storage and cloud storage

### Delete Watchlist Items
1. Open the INDI CHARTS app
2. Go to the Watchlist screen
3. Remove items you no longer want to track
4. Changes will sync across all your devices

### Disable Notifications
1. Open the INDI CHARTS app
2. Go to Settings
3. Disable push notifications
4. Your FCM token will remain but notifications will be disabled

## After Account Deletion

- ✅ You will no longer receive push notifications
- ✅ All your alerts will be permanently deleted
- ✅ Your account will be removed from our systems
- ✅ You can create a new account at any time using the same or different email address
- ⚠️ **Note**: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please ensure you have exported any data you want to keep before requesting deletion.

## Data Export (Before Deletion)

If you want to export your data before deletion:

1. **Alerts**: View all your alerts in the app's Alerts screen (you can take screenshots)
2. **Watchlist**: View your watchlist in the app's Watchlist screen
3. **Settings**: Your preferences are stored locally and will be deleted with app data

**Note**: Currently, there is no automated data export feature. You can manually view and screenshot your data before deletion.
